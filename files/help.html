CASINO Setup: help

How to register for downloading CASINO source code?

For the first time, you need to register on the CASINO download page. Please choose 'CASINO 2.12.1 standard version' at the time of registration. When you complete the registration, a temporary link for the download will appear at the bottom of the page. You can copy and paste this link into the setup GUI.

From the next time, you can use your username and password for downloading the source code.
